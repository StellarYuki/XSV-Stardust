Ship's Databanks

Astral Supply Co.

An interstellar medium-sized freight company specializing in hazardous materials. Family-owned by the Tora-katasu clan. They operate a private fleet of transport and security ships, maintaining the Night Star and Day Star stations.

ISRB (Independent Services Review Board)

An independent organization that handles contracts, licenses, and rights to legitimize mercenary operations. Breaking an ISRB contract classifies a crew as "rogues."

The Setrean Species

A resilient species inhabiting Gastron in the Brunhilde system. They utilize advanced robotics (10-20 meter mechs) and heavily armored, sensor-baffling starships powered by ion propulsion.

Official Roleplay Guide & Ship Rules

Dress Code & Uniforms

We are open to most uniforms! While we have "official" uniforms, you may wear what you like as long as it stays within the sci-fi/Trek theme. Civilian attire is allowed off-duty.

• Females: Short skirts, thigh-highs, bodysuits, and form-fitting attire are highly encouraged!
• Males: For men... pants. XD (Bodysuit uniforms are highly encouraged as well!)

Shuttle Operations & IC Protocols

1. Alarms & Red Alerts: When the alarm sounds, return to your duty station immediately. All personal activities are suspended.

2. Resource Conservation: Lavatory and sonic shower usage is strictly rationed. Access is logged. Holding it is part of the job, and unauthorized use will result in IC reprimands.

3. Cramped Quarters: Keep your gear stowed. If left out, Command will place it in the community locker or recycler.

OOC Rules & Consent

• Adult Themes: Strictly 18+. Lewd scenes/ERP allowed, but keep them out of main open areas during active missions. Getting caught by an officer means IC consequences!
• Consent: Required before combat, sexual touching, mutilation, or perma-death.
• No God-Modding: Your character is not invincible. You must have weaknesses.
