HOME STATION // STATUS

WELCOME ABOARD THE XSV STARDUST

We are an independent mercenary crew operating a heavily modified Type-17 Argo-class shuttle. Originally designed to carry a cargo buggy, the aft section has been completely retrofitted to support deep-space, long-duration roleplay under the Independent Services Review Board (ISRB).

CURRENT DIRECTIVE: OPERATION ABRUIX

Target: Var Lupra System (Planet Pareah)

Objective: Navigate the hazardous Varix Gas Clouds, survive the toxic atmosphere and Gy'zoma beasts of Pareah, and secure the ancient "Abruix" technology.

Status: Preparing for expedition. Taking on secondary ISRB contracts to fund shielding upgrades.

STARDUST RULES // PROTOCOLS

OFFICIAL ROLEPLAY GUIDE & SHIP RULES

This is a strictly 18+ environment. We encourage collaborative, creative storytelling where everyone gets to contribute!

DEPARTMENTS & COLORS

  • BLUE: Command & CONN (The leaders and pilots)
  • RED: Security & Tactical (The protectors and weapons specialists)
  • YELLOW: Engineering & Operations (The fixers and system mechanics)
  • BURGUNDY: Science & Medical (The researchers and healers)

DRESS CODE & UNIFORMS

We are open to most uniforms as long as it stays within the sci-fi/Trek theme. Civilian attire is allowed off-duty.

  • Females: Short skirts, thigh-highs, bodysuits, and form-fitting attire are highly encouraged!
  • Males: Pants. Bodysuit uniforms are highly encouraged as well!

Official Female Duty Uniforms: Jazabelle 'Deep Space 69', Jazabelle 'To Boldly Go', [JRF] Uniform - 2360s

Official Male Duty Uniforms: Mercer Fleet Officer, WW 2371 Uniform, JRF 2360s Uniform

Official Equipment and Rank Pips: TerraCo Rank Pips, [JRF] Tricorder, [JRF] Med Kit, [JRF] Phaser Sidearm, [JRF] Phaser Rifle

SHUTTLE OPERATIONS & IC PROTOCOLS

  • ALARMS & RED ALERTS: Return to duty station immediately.
  • RESOURCE CONSERVATION: Lavatory and sonic shower usage is strictly rationed.
  • REPLICATOR RATIONS: Limited to basic rations, water, and coffee.
  • CRAMPED QUARTERS: Keep personal gear stowed in designated bunk area.
  • MAINTENANCE DUTY: Everyone is expected to help maintain the ship.

OOC RULES, LIMITS & CONSENT

  • ADULT THEMES & ERP: Allowed, but keep out of main open areas during active missions.
  • CONSENT IS REQUIRED: For combat, sexual touching, mutilation, perma-death, or permanent character damage.
  • NO GOD-MODDING OR POWER-GAMING: Characters must have weaknesses.
  • NO META-GAMING: Do not use OOC info for IC situations.
  • CONSEQUENCES: IC actions have consequences.
  • IC vs. OOC ETIQUETTE: Keep OOC chatter to a minimum. Use (( like this )) for OOC.
  • APPEARANCE: No child/underage avatars. Realistic sci-fi appearance. No excessive glow/particles.
  • NO GRIEFING OR SPAMMING: Do not use disruptive objects/HUDs.
  • HAVE FUN: Tell a great story together!
