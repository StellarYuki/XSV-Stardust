HOME STATION // STATUS

WELCOME ABOARD THE XSV STARDUST

We are an independent mercenary crew operating a heavily modified Type-17 Argo-class shuttle. Originally designed to carry a cargo buggy, the aft section has been completely retrofitted to support deep-space, long-duration roleplay under the Independent Services Review Board (ISRB).

CURRENT DIRECTIVE: OPERATION ABRUIX

Target: Var Lupra System (Planet Pareah)

Objective: Navigate the hazardous Varix Gas Clouds, survive the toxic atmosphere and Gy'zoma beasts of Pareah, and secure the ancient "Abruix" technology.

Status: Preparing for expedition. Taking on secondary ISRB contracts to fund shielding upgrades.

STARDUST RULES // PROTOCOLS

OOC & IC GUIDELINES

This is a strictly 18+ environment. We encourage collaborative, creative storytelling where everyone gets to contribute!

  • IC Protocols: Alarms & Red Alerts suspend all personal activities. Replicator rations and sonic showers are strictly rationed.
  • OOC Limits: Consent is required for combat, perma-death, or severe alterations. No god-modding or meta-gaming.
  • Dress Code: Standard XSV Stardust uniforms required on duty. Civilian attire allowed off-duty.
  • Rank Pips: We use the JRF Equipment HUD system (6-tier pip system).

DEPARTMENT COLORS

  • BLUE: Command & CONN (Leaders and pilots)
  • RED: Security & Tactical (Protectors and weapons)
  • YELLOW: Engineering & Operations (Fixers and mechanics)
  • BURGUNDY: Science & Medical (Researchers and healers)
